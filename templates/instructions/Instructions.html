Instructions for part 1

During part 1, you will be playing a game indepdendently with a bot. Your task is to earn as many points as you can across 5 rounds. Each round, you will be given 10 points and then must decide how much of this to send to the bot (you will get to keep what you don't send).

The bot will then send bonus points back to you, which vary depending on your choice.

The points you earn in this game will determine your bonus at the end of the experiment.

When you are ready proceed, click "Begin" below.
